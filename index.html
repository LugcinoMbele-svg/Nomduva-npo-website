Meet Our Leadership

Dedicated professionals committed to transforming communities

Busisiwe Ntuli

Founder & Executive Director

Busisiwe is the visionary Founder of the Nomduva Community Empowerment Project. She established the organisation to create opportunities for young people and promote sustainable social development. Her leadership is driven by a commitment to uplifting disadvantaged communities through education, skills development, and youth empowerment.

Through initiatives such as Ikhaya Golden Mic, she discovers, nurtures, and promotes young talent while helping young people reach their full potential.
